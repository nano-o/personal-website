I sometimes (co-)organize workshops:

* On October 27th, 2025, [Stephan Merz](https://members.loria.fr/Stephan.Merz/) and I co-organized the [12th Workshop on Formal Reasoning in Distributed Algorithms](https://frida-2025.github.io/) (FRIDA), colocated with [DISC 2025](https://www.disc-conference.org/wp/disc2025/) in Berlin, Germany. Video recordings are available on YouTube: [Bill Roscoe](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bmznTzJqUH8), [Ivana Bocevska](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WgPr_BDOWYY), [Juan Villacis](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s5di8St-Hvo), [Raïssa Nataf](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MfTtqXqNBm8), [Stefan Schmid](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3FW3Y9KUviY), [Nathalie Bertrand](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9TxM8YsMr7g), [Gregory Chockler](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mwhsY8H0KG4), [Isabelle Coget](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8oxpeO0JjyE), and [Hagit Attiya](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sFCjre8yLlI).
* On July 23rd, 2024, I co-organizing the [Workshop on Formal Reasoning in Distributed Algorithms](https://frida-2024.github.io) (FRIDA) together with [Swen Jacobs](https://cispa.de/en/people/swen.jacobs/).

The recordings are [available on Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLmr3tp_7-7GjHjTeBSdTu5P15Wde3YMf0).

You can find the videos of the talks on the [QONFEST YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCK9p1Z8nIPTP4Uv5Qodb1og/videos) (search for FRIDA in video titles).
